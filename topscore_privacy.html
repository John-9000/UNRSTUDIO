Privacy Policy for TopScore

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Welcome to TopScore. This Privacy Policy explains how TopScore collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

TopScore does not require users to create an account or provide personal information such as a real name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

1. Information We Collect

TopScore may collect limited information necessary for app functionality, including:

  • Match predictions submitted by users
  • Points, rankings, and leaderboard data
  • A randomly generated player name used for leaderboard participation
  • Anonymous identifiers used by Firebase to associate predictions and leaderboard data with an app installation
  • Advertising-related identifiers and ad interaction data processed by Google AdMob

The randomly generated player name is not intended to identify you personally and may be reset if the app is uninstalled.

2. Firebase Services

TopScore uses Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore to support predictions and leaderboards.

Firebase Authentication may create an anonymous identifier so the app can associate prediction and leaderboard data with an app installation. Users are not asked to create an account, sign in, or provide personal login details.

Firebase Firestore is used to store prediction results, points, rankings, and related leaderboard information.

3. Advertising

TopScore uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, diagnostic information, and ad interaction data. This data is processed by Google according to Google's privacy policies.

You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Notifications

TopScore may display local notifications on your device, such as reminders related to upcoming matches.

These notifications are generated locally on your device. TopScore does not use location data, contacts, camera, microphone, or files for notification functionality.

5. How We Use Information

Information collected by TopScore is used to:

  • Save and display match predictions
  • Calculate points and rankings
  • Provide leaderboard functionality
  • Display rewarded ads

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Some data may be processed by trusted third-party services used by the app, including Google Firebase and Google AdMob. These services process data as necessary to provide app functionality, advertising, security, and fraud prevention.

7. Data Retention

Prediction and leaderboard data may be stored for as long as necessary to provide app functionality, maintain rankings, and support the competition features of TopScore.

If the app is uninstalled, the randomly generated player name and anonymous app installation data may no longer be available on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

TopScore is not directed toward children under the age required by local laws for independent consent.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information used by TopScore. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app, on our website, or through the Google Play Store listing.
