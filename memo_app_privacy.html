Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Memo App handles information when you use the app. Memo App is a notes and reminders app that allows you to write notes and set reminders for yourself.

Summary: Memo App is designed to keep your notes and reminders on your device. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information You Provide

When using Memo App, you may choose to create notes, checklists, reminder text, dates, times, tags, or other content. This content is created by you and is used only to provide the app's note-taking and reminder features.

2. How Your Information Is Stored

Memo App stores your notes and reminders locally on your device, unless a future version of the app clearly states otherwise. We do not operate a server that receives or stores your notes.

If you uninstall the app, your data may be deleted depending on your device settings, Android backup settings, and app storage behavior.

3. Notifications and Reminders

Memo App may use Android notification, alarm, or reminder features to alert you at the date and time you choose. These features are used only to deliver reminders you create in the app.

Reminder delivery may depend on your device settings, battery optimization, notification permissions, Do Not Disturb mode, system restrictions, and other Android behavior outside the app's full control.

4. Permissions

Memo App may request certain permissions when needed for app functionality, such as:

  • Notifications: to show reminder alerts.
  • Alarms or exact alarm access: to schedule reminders more precisely, where supported by Android.

The exact permissions requested may vary depending on your device, Android version, and app version.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your notes or reminders with third parties.

6. Analytics and Advertising

Memo App does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising services. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is collected and how it is used.

7. Children's Privacy

Memo App is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored by the app. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure, including using a device lock screen and protecting access to your phone.

9. Your Choices

You can delete notes and reminders inside the app where deletion features are available. You can also manage app permissions, notifications, and storage through your Android device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the updated version of this page, with a new "Last updated" date.
