Privacy Policy for TopScore

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Welcome to TopScore. This Privacy Policy explains how TopScore collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app to view football match schedules, scores, match details, and reminders.

1. Information We Collect

TopScore may collect limited information necessary for app functionality, including:

  • App preferences, such as language, theme
  • Match reminder settings created by the user
  • App performance and crash information
  • Advertising and analytics data provided by third-party services

TopScore does not require users to create an account.

2. Match Scores and Fixtures

TopScore displays football match schedules, teams, venues, live scores, results, and related match information.

The app may retrieve match data from cloud services so the schedule, scores, and match details can stay up to date.

3. Local App Preferences

TopScore may store app preferences on your device, such as selected language, theme, and other settings needed to operate the app.

These preferences are used to provide the app experience you selected.

4. Advertising

TopScore may display optional advertisements using third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

These providers may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage statistics
  • Diagnostic information

Advertising may be personalized depending on your consent settings and regional regulations.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

The app may use analytics and crash reporting services to improve stability and performance.

Collected information may include:

  • Device type
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Language
  • Crash logs
  • General usage statistics

7. Data Storage

TopScore may use cloud services such as Google Firebase to provide and update match schedules, scores, results, and related app data.

Some app preferences may be stored locally on your device. Cloud services and third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

TopScore is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

9. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google AdMob
  • Android system services

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

10. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable notifications
  • Reset advertising identifiers from Android settings
  • Limit ad personalization through your device settings
  • Clear app data or uninstall the app at any time

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes become effective when the updated policy is published.

If TopScore adds new features that collect or use additional data, such as interactive community features, this policy and the app's store data disclosures should be updated before those features are made available.
