Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VividCam handles user information. VividCam is a camera application designed to help users take photos on their Android device.

Information We Collect

VividCam does not currently collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Camera Permission

VividCam requires access to your device camera so that you can take photos. The camera permission is used only for camera functionality inside the app.

Photos and Media

Photos taken with VividCam are stored locally on your device. VividCam does not upload your photos to any server.

Advertising

VividCam does not currently display ads.

In-App Purchases

VividCam may include free and premium features in the future. If premium features are added, purchases will be handled through Google Play.

Third-Party Services

VividCam does not currently use third-party advertising or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

VividCam does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
